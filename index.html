Hi! I am a final-year PhD student at University of Science and Technology of China, advised by Prof. Yan Chen. Previously, I received my Master's and Bachelor's degrees in Communication Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.

Research

My research focuses on computational imaging, mmWave radar imaging, and wireless sensing. My recent works explore:

• mmWave radar imaging / mmWave radar point cloud reconstruction (TIP'24, TMC'24, CE'24, ICASSP'24)

• Advancing non-line-of-sight imaging techniques (TIP'22, TIP'25, ICASSP'25, ICME'25)

• Developing security measures for wireless sensing systems (TIFS'25)

• Applying wireless sensing to other applications, e.g., biomedical sensing and human-centric perception (Nature Communications'25, ICLR'25, WCSP'25)
